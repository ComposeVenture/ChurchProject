The scripture say give and you will be given, the measurement you used to others, and it will be measured to you too: Luke 6:38. Above all else, giving draws our heart to God, Mt 6: 21 say: for where your treasure is, there your heart will be also. When you give to God to support Christ’s work, you keep your money in the heavenly bank or treasure. You’re giving expressing your deep love for him. Nothing on this planet in more important than understanding and knowing Christ well. Your giving through this ministry , gives a great opportunity to store up your treasure in heaven, where neither moth nor rust destroys and where thieves do not break in or steal’ our Lord Jesus confirmed us that the heaven as a Heavenly bank where our money are being store every day. OUR GIVING SHOULD BE SACRIFICIAL

For God so loved the world and gave us his son who died on the cross because of our sin, God demonstrated his divine love for mankind through offering his only son to die for the whole world, so that through him all might be saved from the repercussion of sin. John 3:16-20. Because God loved us so much and gave his son to die, indeed he died for us. This only sets a model and principle of giving to others as one way sacrificing ourselves to other through our generosity. In the book of 1 John 3:16-18, it says, this is how we know what love is: Jesus Christ laid down his life for us. And we ought to lay down our lives for brothers and sisters. If anyone has material possessions and sees a brother or sister in need but has no pity on them, how can the love of God be in that person? Dear children, let us not love with words or speech but with actions and truth.
